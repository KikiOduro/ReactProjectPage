About Us

We are a passionate team of boba lovers who believe every cup should tell a story. Born out of a love for bold flavors and good vibes, our shop brings you handcrafted teas made with the freshest ingredients , because you deserve nothing less. From classic milk teas to fruity blends, every sip is made with care, creativity, and a whole lot of love. Come as you are, stay for the boba.
